Key features

Discover the powerful capabilities that make Angry Data Scanner the ideal solution for sensitive data discovery

Ranking

Scanner shows high-value files first, helping you prioritize the most critical findings. The intelligent ranking system analyzes data sensitivity and presents results in order of importance.

View scanning history

Track all your previous scans with detailed history. Review past results, compare scans over time, and maintain a complete audit trail of your data discovery activities.

Export results

Download results in a CSV file for further analysis, reporting, or integration with other tools. Export includes all detected data types, locations, and metadata.

Schedule scans

Automate your scanning process with scheduled scans. Set up recurring scans to continuously monitor your data sources and ensure ongoing compliance.

Configurable matchers

Configure PII, PCI DSS and other matchers to match your specific compliance requirements. Enable or disable detection patterns based on your needs.

Multiple file formats

Configure file formats (pdf, excel, etc.) to scan. Support for MS Office, Open Office, Adobe PDF, archives, and plain text files. Customize which formats to include in your scans.
